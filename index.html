I started my career navigating real oceans — as a Merchant Navy Officer with MOL Maritime, one of the world's largest shipping companies (~800 ships, $10B revenue). Over 6 years at sea, I led crisis response during engine failures that could have caused $50M in damages, redesigned piracy safety protocols adopted across 6 ships, and earned the Annual Safety Award across a fleet of 40 vessels.

That foundation in high-stakes, precision decision-making became the operating system I carried into logistics at Blowhorn and Delhivery (scaling warehouse infrastructure to $135M in synergies), aerospace consulting at Cascade / IMP Group (a 40% turnaround reduction contributing to a $270M NATO contract win), and strategy consulting at OLG (securing CEO buy-in for a $25M revenue pipeline).

Now completing my MBA at Rotman, University of Toronto (GMAT 740 — 97th percentile, Top 25%, $30K merit scholarship), I bring the same navigational discipline to complex organizational challenges across strategy, operations, and transformation.

Navigation Principles

How I think about complex problems

🧭
Read the signals early

Sailors watched birds and clouds to anticipate storms. I noticed a barcode placement pattern causing 30% sorter rejection before the VP's 3-day deadline — solved to 4%. Weak signals reveal deeper problems.

Applied at Delhivery · Root cause analysis

⭐
Find your North Star

Strategy only works when decisions align with a clear long-term direction. Reducing aircraft from 6 to 4 concurrent was painful short-term — but built trust and secured the $270M NATO contract.

Applied at Cascade Aerospace · USAF program

📐
Measure before acting

Navigators used sextants and charts. I re-visualized TAT data on a Gantt chart and found the bottleneck everyone had missed. The right data, presented differently, reveals what standard reports hide.

Applied at Cascade Aerospace · Process optimization

⚓
Stay steady in uncertainty

When a ship's engine failed at sea with $50M at risk, clear-headedness was the only resource. I led 5 cross-functional teams, stabilized the situation, and earned the Annual Safety Award across 40 ships.

Applied at MOL Maritime · Engine failure crisis

🌊
Respect the system

Oceans and organizations are complex systems that don't respond to simple fixes. Reframing a binary app KPI into tiered percentages — without touching underlying incentives — raised usage from 38% to 95%.

Applied at Blowhorn · Operations transformation

🔭
Look beyond the horizon

The best navigators simulate futures before they arrive. At OLG, I modeled a new revenue vertical modeled on India's Lucky Yatra before anyone had asked the question — and secured CEO buy-in for a $25M pilot.

Applied at OLG · Partnership strategy
